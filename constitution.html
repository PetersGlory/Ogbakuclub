OGBAKU CLUB CONSTITUTION

THE CONSTITUTION:
  1. ARTICLE 1 (NAME)
    The Club shall be known and addressed by the name "OGBAKU CLUB"

  2. ARTICLE 2 (MOTTO & LOGO)
    The Motto of the Club shall be "PEACE AND DEVELOPMENT".

    The Club's logo is comprises of a green leaf, a book, a dove and an eighteen-spiked wheel.

    The green leaf represents agriculture, which is the mainstay of Ogbaku people. The book stands for education, the prime mover of development. The dove is a symbol of peace, and the eighteen-spiked wheel denotes the much needed development of the eighteen villages that make up Ogbaku.
    The office and registered address of the Club shall be 7th Avenue, 721 Road,E Close, House 12 Festac Town, Lagos,
