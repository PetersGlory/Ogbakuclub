About Ogbaku Club

Ogbaku is essentially a peaceful, closely knit,agrarian society at the mid level of infrastructural development and cosmopolitanism in MBAITOLI local government area of Imo state. It's located approximately 10km from the state capital, Owerri.

About the year 1981, some bright-eyed and patriotic members of the Ogbaku elite community in the quest for educational, political, economic, and social development in the community, as well as ensure peaceful co-existence decided to found "OGBAKU CLUB " with, as the title suggests, Ogbaku as the centrepiece of it's existence. >>Read more

Benefits for Ogbaku

This is enshrined in the objectives of the club which focus on Ogbaku, among which are:
Promoting physical, social and economic development amongst members and the society in general.
Promoting and organising social, cultural, education and sporting activities between the Club, other Clubs and Organisations.
Creation of a dynamic and non partisan body with the sole aim of reconciling all conflicting and divergent groups in Ogbaku, so as to obtain unity of action and maximum utilisation of human and economic resources of Ogbaku people in development programmes.
Contributing towards the uplifting and enhancement of the life of the needy and less privileged in the society.

A platform to associate, network and rub minds with Ogbaku elites of various callings and proffessions towards assisting to develop Ogbaku.

Opportunity to multiply members' individual efforts, through the synergy that results from the collective, modest individual efforts to develop Ogbaku.
