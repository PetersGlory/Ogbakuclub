BENEFITS FOR OGBAKU


This is enshrined in the objectives of the club which focus on Ogbaku, among which are:

  1. Promoting physical, social and economic developments amongst members and the society in general.

  2. Creation of a dynamic and non partisan body with the sole aim of reconciling all conflicting and divergent groups in Ogbaku, so as to obtain unity of action and maximum utilisation of human and economic resources of Ogbaku people in development programmes.

  3. Promoting and organising social, cultural, education and sporting activities between the Club, other Clubs and Organisations.

  4. Contributing towards the uplifting and enhancement of the life of the needy and less privileged in the society.

  5. Promoting and assisting the education of all Ogbaku children through formal, non-formal, technical education etc and by direct intervention and or in liaison with all educational institutions/providers within Ogbaku Clan.

  6. Promoting and coordinating cordial relationships between Ogbaku Clan/citizens and all institutions and organizations within Ogbaku clan.

  7. Enhancing, promoting and preservation of all good cultural practices in Ogbaku clan.
