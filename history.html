Our Story

Ogbaku is essentially a peaceful, closely knit, agrarian society at the mid level of infrastructural development and cosmopolitanism in MBAITOLI local government area of Imo state. It's located approximately 10km from the state capital, Owerri.

About the year 1981, some bright-eyed and patriotic members of the Ogbaku elite community in the quest for educational, political, economic, and social development in the community, as well as ensure peaceful co-existence decided to found "OGBAKU CLUB "with, as the title suggests, Ogbaku as the centrepiece of its existence.

The club which started with mostly Lagos based members has since extended to Owerri and Abuja, with rapidly increasing membership and appeal. This club has remained the flagship of educational and economic development in the town.
